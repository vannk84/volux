Legal · Privacy Policy

Your data stays with you.

Volux does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything lives in your browser — no servers, no accounts, no telemetry.

Overview

Volux is a browser extension for per-domain and per-tab volume control. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

The short version

The Volux extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your volume preferences live in your browser’s local storage — on your device, never on ours.

Data Collection

Extension vs website

Important distinction:

  • Volux extension — no tracking, no analytics, no data collection of any kind.
  • This website — uses Microsoft Clarity for basic visitor analytics (page views only). This does NOT affect the extension. Your volume settings and browsing activity remain completely private in the extension itself.

What we store locally

The following data is stored only on your device using browser local storage:

  • Volume preferences — your chosen volume levels for each domain.
  • License key — if you activate a Pro license (stored locally for validation).
  • Domain list — the websites you’ve added for volume control.

What we do NOT collect

  • No personal information.
  • No browsing history.
  • No analytics or tracking in the extension.
  • No cookies for advertising.
  • No data shared with third parties.

Permissions explained

Volux requires certain browser permissions to function:

PermissionWhy it’s needed
tabsTo identify which tabs contain audio and read domain names.
storageTo save your volume preferences locally.
host_permissionsTo control audio on any website you choose.

License validation

When you activate a Pro license:

  • Your license key is validated against LemonSqueezy’s API.
  • Only the license key is sent (no personal data).
  • The validation confirms the license is valid.
  • Your license status is stored locally afterwards.

Third-party services

LemonSqueezy — payment processing for Pro licenses (only if you choose to purchase). Their privacy policy: lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.

Microsoft Clarity — page-view analytics for this website only. Does not run inside the extension. Privacy policy: clarity.microsoft.com/terms.

Data security

  • All data remains on your device.
  • No external servers store your information.
  • No accounts or sign-ups required.

Children’s privacy

Volux does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy:

  • Email: support@devlifeeasy.com
  • GitHub: github.com/vannk84/volux/issues
Bottom line

Your data stays on your device. We don’t collect, track, or sell anything.
