14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Not satisfied? Get a full refund within 14 days of purchase. No questions asked.

Our Commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with Volux Pro. If for any reason you're not happy with your purchase, we offer a straightforward refund policy.

Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 14 days of your purchase date
  • This is your first refund request for Volux

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, simply:

  1. Send an email to support@devlifeeasy.com
  2. Include your order number or the email used for purchase
  3. Briefly mention why you'd like a refund (optional, but helps us improve)

We typically process refund requests within 1-3 business days. Once approved, the refund will be credited to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

What Happens After a Refund

After a refund is processed:

  • Your Pro license will be deactivated
  • You will revert to the Free version (limited to 2 domains)
  • Your volume settings and preferences will remain intact

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds may not be granted in the following cases:

  • Requests made after 14 days from the purchase date
  • Multiple refund requests from the same user
  • Evidence of license key abuse or sharing
  • Chargebacks initiated without first contacting us

Try Before You Buy

We encourage you to try the Free version of Volux before purchasing Pro. The Free version includes all core features with a limit of 2 domains.
