# Privacy Policy for Volux

**Last Updated:** March 2025

## Overview

Volux is a browser extension for per-domain volume control. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

## Data Collection

**Volux does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What We Store Locally

The following data is stored **only on your device** using browser local storage:

- **Volume preferences** - Your chosen volume levels for each domain
- **License key** - If you activate a Pro license (stored locally for validation)
- **Domain list** - The websites you've added for volume control

### What We Do NOT Collect

- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No analytics or tracking
- No cookies for advertising
- No data shared with third parties

## Permissions Explained

Volux requires certain browser permissions to function:

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `tabs` | To identify which tabs contain audio and get domain names |
| `storage` | To save your volume preferences locally |
| `scripting` | To inject the volume control script into web pages |
| `host_permissions` | To control audio on any website you choose |

## License Validation

When you activate a Pro license:
- Your license key is validated against LemonSqueezy's API
- Only the license key is sent (no personal data)
- The validation confirms the license is valid
- Your license status is stored locally

## Third-Party Services

- **LemonSqueezy** - Payment processing for Pro licenses (only if you purchase)
  - Privacy Policy: https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy

## Data Security

- All data remains on your device
- No external servers store your information
- No accounts or sign-ups required

## Children's Privacy

Volux does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date.
