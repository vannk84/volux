## Key Improvements

### 1. ✅ CRITICAL: Added Permissions Section (Chrome Requirement)

**OLD:** ❌ Missing entirely
**NEW:** ✅ Detailed section

```
🔐 PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED

Volux needs certain permissions to work. Here's exactly why:

• Storage Permission
  Saves your volume preferences locally on YOUR device.

• Tabs Permission
  Detects which tabs have audio and gets website names.

• Access to Websites
  Controls audio/video elements on sites you choose.
```

**Why this matters:**
- Addresses Chrome rejection reason #1
- Required after 3 rejections
- Builds trust with users
- Shows transparency
